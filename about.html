About MeroTrend

Born in Birtamode, built for Nepal. We're on a mission to make trendy fashion accessible to everyone across the country.

Our Story

MeroTrend started with a simple idea — fashion shouldn't be limited by geography. Growing up in Birtamode, Jhapa, we noticed that the latest fashion trends were always concentrated in big cities like Kathmandu.

We set out to change that. Today, MeroTrend delivers curated, high-quality fashion to all 77 districts of Nepal. From the Terai plains to the Himalayan foothills, we bring trending styles right to your doorstep.

Our team handpicks every item in our collection — ensuring quality, style, and affordability. Whether you're looking for everyday basics or something special, MeroTrend has you covered.
